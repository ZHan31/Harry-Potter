Harry Potter

Harry Potter is the main protagonist of the series. He is a wizard who survived an attack by the evil wizard Voldemort when he was a baby, and he becomes a hero in the wizarding world as he battles Voldemort and his followers.

Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger is one of Harry's best friends. She is a talented witch who is very smart and always eager to learn. She helps Harry and Ron in their adventures and becomes a key player in the fight against Voldemort.

Ron Weasley

Ron Weasley is another one of Harry's best friends. He comes from a large, poor wizarding family, and he is often insecure about his abilities. However, he is loyal and brave, and he helps Harry and Hermione in their adventures.

Albus Dumbledore

Albus Dumbledore is the headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He is one of the greatest wizards of all time, and he is a mentor to Harry as he faces Voldemort. He is wise and powerful, but he also has some dark secrets from his past.

Volan-De-Mort

Volan de Mort, also known as Lord Voldemort, is a complex and enigmatic character within the Harry Potter series. He is portrayed as a tall, thin man with pale, snake-like features, including slits for nostrils, blood-red eyes, and a bald head. His appearance is a reflection of his dark and twisted nature, instilling fear and intimidation in those who encounter him.

Snape

Snape was actually a lot more than just a mean professor to Harry. He had a seriously complicated relationship with Harry's mom, Lily. See, Snape was head over heels in love with Lily back when they were in school together. But things got messed up when Snape started hanging out with the wrong crowd and joined Voldemort's gang, the Death Eaters. That drove a huge wedge between him and Lily.
